Creative Workflow

  1. IDEA: Everything begins with a an emotion.
  2. Characters: Who these character are connected with perticular emotion.
  3. Message: What message i want to give to the world from my story.
  4. World: A world that breathes the same air as the characters.
  5. Story:
    • Why I Write: To reflect pain, hope, and healing.
    • Who’s In It: A deeply flawed soul with purpose.
    • Where: Settings that mirror emotions.
    • Structure: Plot --> Start --> End. Then flood it with emotion.
  6. Emotions: Not fluff. They’re the gameplay. Fear. Grief. Hope. Love. All real.
  7. Mechanics: It depends upon the story. Realism is what is chase. RESEARCH RESEARCH RESEARCH JUST DON'T MAKE IT LOOK REAL MAKE IT FEEL REAL!
  8. Final Steps: First, the story layout. Then the Game Design Document (GDD). Lastly, a thorough story outline that includes all the subtleties. We don't begin developing the game until after that.
  9. The Goal: To create a hauntingly beautiful experience that lingers long after the game is over.
